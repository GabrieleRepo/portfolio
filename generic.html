Overview

I'm an Italian Bachelor's graduate in Biomedical Engineering, currently studying and working on Data Science projects. My journey into data science began during my curricular internship, where I used MATLAB to identify the P wave features from electrocardiographic signals. I was amazed by the wealth of information that can derive from such a small and intricate signal. This experience helped me realize my interest in Signal Analysis and extracting meaningful insights from data.

Since then, I've pursued additional studies in Signal and Image Processing, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI, which led me to pursue a Master's in Data Analysis. I am expanding my knowledge by keeping up with the latest advancements in AI and machine learning technologies with a Master's in Data Science through the start2impact online learning platform.

In addition to my technical background, I also worked as a lifeguard, which taught me the importance of making quick, data-driven decisions under pressure. This experience sharpened my ability to remain calm and effective in high-stress situations, a skill I apply when working with complex data challenges.

During my studies, I completed several practical projects that honed my ability to work with complex data and developed a keen eye for patterns and trends. I also gained valuable experience in data management and statistical analysis and learned to use SQL, Tableau, and Python.

I aspire to leverage data and machine learning algorithms to help companies optimize production processes, enabling informed decision-making and time savings. I am actively seeking entry-level positions and hands-on training opportunities in Data Analysis and Data Science, aiming to launch a dynamic new phase in my professional career.

I am excited to collaborate on data-driven projects and help companies unlock the potential of their data. Feel free to contact me using the information below.

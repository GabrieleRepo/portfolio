Overview

I'm an Italian Bachelor's graduate in Biomedical Engineering, currently studying and working on projects in Data Science. I enjoy spending my leisure time swimming, visiting the beach, and learning new things online.

How did I end up interested in Data Analysis? Well, it hasn't been easy. I didn't have a predefined dream, which made things much harder. I had to figure out what I truly enjoyed. I started my academic journey with a Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering, carrying out an internship focused on analyzing ECG signals with MATLAB.

Later, I pursued a Master's degree in the same field, thinking it was the right path, but I didn't feel fulfilled. I realized I was lacking motivation and purpose during the pandemic lockdown. Despite the challenges, I seized the opportunity to rediscover my interests. I revisited my past experiences and realized how my internship proved to be an incredibly enlightening experience, teaching me the importance of signal analysis in uncovering meaningful details.

Starting from here, I decided to delve into data analysis's field. Meanwhile, I obtained the professional qualification to work as a lifeguard and began studying SQL and Python as hobbies. During my studies, I completed several practical projects, where I honed my ability to work with complex data and developed a keen eye for patterns and trends. I also gained experience in data management, statistical analysis, and decision-making under high-pressure situations.

I aspire to leverage data and machine learning algorithms to help companies optimize production processes, enabling informed decision-making and time savings. I am actively seeking entry-level positions and hands-on training opportunities in Data Analysis and Data Science, aiming to launch a dynamic new phase in my professional career.

Thank you for listening to my story. I am excited to collaborate on exciting data-driven projects in the future. Feel free to reach out through my contact information below.
